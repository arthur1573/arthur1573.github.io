about me












I remember when I was in elementary school.

Slogans prohibiting burning wheat straws are posted everywhere,

always seen during the wheat harvest season.

I don't understand why it is forbidden.

I didn't understand a little until one afternoon:

That afternoon the school suddenly notified that there would be no classes in the afternoon.

This kind of unprecedented thing is only possible if the school's electric room blows up.

So after lunch, I go out for a walk.

Unexpectedly, the sky turned dark.

I take out my watch to check the time.

Make sure it's two o'clock in the afternoon.

then everything around is dyed gold.

Gradually turns orange, then turns to dark yellow.

Soon yellow to the end, only black remains.

In dark midnight has moonlight,

I was walking on the road but I couldn't see my fingers.

That's the darkest black I've ever seen.

After about 40 minutes,

The sky only leaked the color of burning clouds.

It turns out that darkness does not only exist in the most hidden corners.

This place I lived in is full of dark, so I want to run.
